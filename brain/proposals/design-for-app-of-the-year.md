# Design bar: aim for App Store featuring (App of the Year/Month)

**The idea:** Set the design quality bar high enough that the app earns an Apple
feature — **App of the Year / App of the Month** on the App Store. Design isn't a
finish; it's the strategy.

**Why / what problem:** An Apple feature is enormous, free distribution — the
single biggest organic growth lever on the store. Directly serves the
[[north-star]]: featuring → installs → money, and a craft-first bar makes the work
fun. Pairs with the [[science-games-for-kids]] direction (Apple regularly features
polished kids/education titles).

**What we'd need to agree on:**
- Commit to design as a first-class pillar, not a layer added at the end.
- What "feature-worthy" means for us (originality, polish, accessibility, the
  Apple design-award criteria) and how we'd build toward it.
- Tradeoff: a high design bar costs time — acceptable against the
  [[budget-constraint]] (free infra, but our hours)?

**Notes / discussion:** Dor's domain ([[dor-role]]). Aspirational target that
shapes scope and quality decisions if the team buys in.
