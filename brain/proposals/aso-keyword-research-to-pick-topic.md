# Method: App Store keyword research to find the topic

**The idea:** Before committing to a specific game/topic, **research App Store
keywords** (ASO) to find a topic with real search demand and beatable competition,
and let that data pick the topic — rather than guessing.

**Why / what problem:** Picking a topic blind is risky. Keyword demand vs.
competition is a cheap, data-driven way to choose where to play and improve
discoverability from day one. Directly serves the [[north-star]] money goal —
finding demand before building.

**What we'd need to agree on:**
- Which (free-tier, per [[budget-constraint]]) ASO tools to use.
- The screening criteria: search volume, difficulty/competition, relevance to
  kids + science.
- Who runs the research and by when.

**Notes / discussion:** Scoped within the [[science-games-for-kids]] domain lean —
this is *how* we'd choose the exact topic inside that space.
