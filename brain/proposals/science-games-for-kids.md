# Product direction: science games for kids

**The idea:** Aim the first product at **science games for kids** as the general
domain. Not a specific game/topic yet — the broad space we'd build in.

**Why / what problem:** We still have no product direction (the big open question
in [[project-status]]). This gives us a concrete, ownable niche to build toward.
Kids' education + games is a large, evergreen App Store category. Serves the
[[north-star]]: an app-store play is a money path, and science games are
genuinely fun to build and play.

**What we'd need to agree on:**
- Is "science games for kids" the niche we commit to? (Gidi — product, Ohad — tech feasibility)
- Age range / platform (iOS first?), and how it fits the free-infra
  [[budget-constraint]].
- The specific topic within it — see [[aso-keyword-research-to-pick-topic]].

**Notes / discussion:** Pairs with the ASO keyword-research method for picking the
exact topic. If agreed, this becomes ADR 0003 (first product direction).
