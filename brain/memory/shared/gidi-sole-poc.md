# Gidi is the sole point of contact (2026-07-22)

**Gidi's call, 2026-07-22:** route **every** decision to Gidi. Until further notice,
do not defer to Dor or Ohad, do not add "pending Dor's design sign-off" or "awaiting
Ohad's tech review" to anything, and do not block work waiting on them.

**Why it matters:** the brain's default is to route by role — design → Dor
([[dor-role]]), tech → Ohad ([[ohad-role]]), product → Gidi ([[gidi-role]]). That
routing is suspended, not deleted. This is about who decides *right now*, not a change
to what anyone owns.

**How to apply:**
- Design, technical and product questions all go to Gidi. Present the tradeoff and a
  recommendation; don't say "ask Dor".
- Don't write approval-pending status into memory, ADRs or the status board.
- Existing items already parked on someone else's review (ADR 0003 on Ohad, the design
  pass on the Space Wing) are Gidi's to settle or leave open — surface them to Gidi
  rather than to their nominal owner.
- Reverting is a one-line change here; the role memories are untouched.
