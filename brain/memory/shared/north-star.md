# 🌟 North Star

The goals that keep us aligned — the thing every decision, idea, and priority
should serve. **Live and evolving.** Last updated: **2026-06-09**.

## Our north star (v0 — starting point)
1. **Make us money.** 💰
2. **Make it fun.** 🎉

## How we use it
- **Every decision, proposal, and priority should serve the north star.** When
  weighing a call or an idea, ask: *does this make us money, and is it fun?* If a
  choice trades one off against the other, name the tradeoff out loud.
- It's the **tie-breaker** when priorities conflict.
- It **evolves.** As we sharpen what "make money" and "fun" mean for us (market,
  product, audience), update this file and bump the date. Keep it short and true —
  a north star you can recite, not a strategy doc.
