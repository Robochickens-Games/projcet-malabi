Follow the canonical skill at `brain/skills/brain-dump/SKILL.md` — read it and run
the full lifecycle: capture → classify → log as a proposal in `brain/proposals/`
(`status: proposed`) → surface for the team → manage review until the team agrees.
Never act on an idea or treat it as decided until it's `agreed`.
