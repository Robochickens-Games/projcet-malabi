Follow the canonical skill at `brain/skills/safe-git/SKILL.md` — the team's plain-language,
no-git-experience-required guide to working together safely: the daily safe loop (sync → small
chunks → save often → share often → end clean), the rules that stop people stepping on each other's
work (pull before start, one chunk at a time, branch for big/risky work, push small & often), the
never-lose-work safety nets, and what to do when git looks scary (STOP and ask the AI — never force,
never discard). It pairs with [[version-manager]] (the AI's automatic git) — this skill is for the
humans; version-manager is what the AI does for them. The AI does the hard parts; the team just
follows the loop and asks for help the moment anything looks off.
